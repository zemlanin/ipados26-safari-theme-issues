This page demonstates issues with <meta name="theme-color"> in Safari on iPadOS* 26 Developer Beta 1, namely:

  • <meta name="theme-color"> does not affect Safari top bar
  • <meta name="theme-color"> does not affect the viewport "background" color when overscrolling

When scrolling the page, page navbar's background and theme colors change:

  • Before scrolling, navbar has a slight dark gradient and the theme color is black to put focus on the hero image
  • After scrolling, navbar and theme are page's background color to blend in
